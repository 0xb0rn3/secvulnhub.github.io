Why We Exist

Built by hunters, for hunters.

Every framework ships with bloat. Every security tool has three layers of abstraction that do nothing except look impressive. SecVulnHub exists to ship clean, purposeful tools that practitioners actually reach for.

Quality over quantity. Tested in the field. Maintained by the community. If it doesn't solve a real problem, it doesn't ship.
